About Bloodware

Bloodware, Inc. is a professional software development and modding studio specializing in high-quality game modifications and software tools. Formerly known as Team Force RBLX, we have reclaimed our original brand name and maintain strict control over all partnerships and endorsements. Any associations or collaborations must be verified by Bloodware employees to be official.

Our Expertise

  • Custom game modifications and tools for developers and gamers.
  • Collaborations with our game development partners: Velxy Studios and Dead Lantern Games.
  • Building and optimizing our games and software using powerful hardware provided by Arza PCs.
  • Focused on innovation, quality, and community-driven development.

Important Notice

Bloodware, Inc. does NOT endorse or partner with any groups or accounts unless explicitly verified by our team. Be wary of impersonators or unverified entities using our name or branding.
