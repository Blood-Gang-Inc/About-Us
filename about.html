About Bloodware

Bloodware is a cutting-edge software development and game modding studio focused on empowering creators through innovation and performance. Founded with a passion for immersive gameplay and secure technology, we deliver tools and experiences that push the boundaries of what’s possible.

Our core strengths include high-performance software, advanced game mods, and hardware solutions tailored for developers and gamers alike.

We take pride in our commitment to privacy, creativity, and a community-driven approach, which is reflected across our projects — including our flagship privacy-focused social platform, Krynet.
